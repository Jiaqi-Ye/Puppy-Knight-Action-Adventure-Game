# Puppy Knight — Action Adventure Game

A young puppy knight awakens in a once-vibrant forest now overtaken by corruption. As nature decays and creatures turn hostile, the knight sets out on a journey to uncover the source of the spreading darkness and restore balance to the land.


## Overview

**Puppy Knight** is a 2D action-adventure game developed in **Unity**, focusing on responsive combat, environmental storytelling, and immersive exploration.

Players traverse interconnected regions of a corrupted forest, encountering enemies, obstacles, and fragments of a world slowly collapsing.

Rather than relying on explicit narration, the game tells its story through atmosphere, visual design, and player interaction.


## Gameplay

### Exploration
- Navigate layered environments with hidden paths and obstacles  
- Discover new areas as you progress deeper into the forest  

### Combat System
- Real-time combat with timing-based attacks  
- Emphasis on positioning, movement, and responsiveness  

### Environmental Interaction
- Interactive objects that respond to player actions  
- Reinforces immersion and gameplay feedback  

### Progression
- Increasing enemy difficulty and environmental challenges  
- Gradual exposure to deeper and more corrupted regions  


## Technical Highlights

- Built with **Unity (C#)**
- Physics-based character movement and collision detection  
- Modular scripting for player control and enemy behaviors  
- Interactive UI system for feedback and game state  
- Scalable project structure for future expansion  


## Design Focus

- Minimalist storytelling through environment and mood  
- Consistent visual tone reflecting decay and restoration  
- Tight gameplay loop combining exploration and combat
